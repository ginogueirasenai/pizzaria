Our story is Your Story

If you love pizza piez, and good food, and good drinks you've come to the right place!

  • I grew up down in the Valley, near Exit 9. My partner Sonny and I had a '69 Chevy with a 396 engine that we built from scratch. We used to take it racing out at the Trestles, right on the edge of town. After those races, I discovered my love for pizza.
  • Back then, Mary and I would often eat at Kinchley's, or we’d drive out of the Valley up to Martio's to enjoy their famous tomato pie. Now, I have my own place—Dino's. It's just the way I like it, and I know you will too. Our menu is packed with great food, and we also serve a wide selection of drinks. For those who love a good bar menu, we’ve got you covered with real, quality drinks. We hope you’ll enjoy plenty of them.
  • Dino's is for people 21 and over at 1524 E Olive Way in Seattle.

Round Weekend at Dino's Pizza

Our dish features a rich tomato sauce as the base, topped with a blend of fresh and aged mozzarella for the perfect balance of creamy and tangy flavors.

We add savory Zoe's bacon, sweet pineapple, and our house-pickled jalapeños, which provide a delightful kick. This combination creates a harmonious mix of flavors that’s both satisfying and unique.

Round White Pie

A perfect blend of fresh ricotta, aged mozzarella, garlic, and Parmesan creates an unmatched flavor profile. Fresh ricotta provides a creamy, light texture, while aged mozzarella adds a robust, chewy element.

Garlic introduces an aromatic, deep flavor, complemented by Parmesan, which imparts a rich, salty note. Together, these ingredients create a delicious and harmonious combination, ideal for enhancing any dish.

Square Cheese Pizza

A delightful blend of tomato sauce, fresh and aged mozzarella, Parmesan, and fresh basil delivers a classic and satisfying flavor profile. The tomato sauce provides a tangy and rich base, while fresh mozzarella adds a creamy, milky texture and aged mozzarella contributes a deeper, more complex flavor with a slightly chewy texture.

Parmesan introduces a savory, salty kick, and fresh basil adds a burst of aromatic, herbal brightness. Together, these ingredients create a well-balanced and flavorful combination that enhances any dish.

Round Pepperoni Pizza

A delicious blend of tomato sauce, fresh and aged mozzarella, Parmesan, and Zoe's pepperoni creates a rich and satisfying flavor profile. The tomato sauce provides a tangy and robust foundation, while fresh mozzarella adds a creamy and melty texture, complemented by the aged mozzarella’s deeper, more complex flavor and slight chewiness.

Parmesan introduces a savory, salty note, and Zoe's pepperoni brings a spicy, flavorful kick with a perfectly crisp texture. Together, these ingredients combine to offer a classic yet distinctive taste experience.

Square Pepperoni Pizza

Our pizza features a mouthwatering blend of tomato sauce, fresh and aged mozzarella, Parmesan, and Zoe's pepperoni. The tomato sauce delivers a vibrant, tangy foundation, perfectly complemented by the creamy smoothness of fresh mozzarella and the rich, robust flavor of aged mozzarella.

Parmesan adds a sharp, salty finish, while Zoe's pepperoni provides a zesty, spicy kick with a satisfying crunch. This combination creates a flavorful and well-rounded pizza that’s sure to please.
